Booking & Rides

Simply enter your trip details in the booking form on our homepage — choose your trip type (One Way, By the Hour, Day Tour, or Airport Transfer), set your route and date, then click "Search Vehicles". Select your vehicle, fill in passenger details, and complete payment. You'll receive an instant confirmation email.
Airport Transfers: 60 minutes of free waiting from the actual landing time of your flight.
All other services: 15 minutes of free waiting per stop.
After the free waiting period, overtime is charged at the vehicle's hourly rate, billed per minute.
≥ 24 hours before pickup: Full refund. You may cancel (modifications require rebooking).
12–24 hours before pickup: 50% refund, cancellation only.
< 12 hours before pickup: No refund, no cancellation.
To cancel, go to My Orders and click "Cancel" next to your booking.
If your group or luggage exceeds the capacity of one vehicle, we'll suggest upgrading to a larger vehicle or splitting into multiple vehicles. Multiple vehicles can be booked on the same order with a combined bill, billed separately per vehicle.

Airport Transfers

We automatically track your flight in real-time. If your flight is delayed, your chauffeur will be informed and will adjust their arrival time accordingly. The 60-minute free waiting period begins from the actual landing time of your flight, not the scheduled time.
Your chauffeur will be waiting at the arrivals hall with a personalized name sign. By default, the sign shows your full name, but you can customize the text (up to 30 characters) during booking. You'll also receive the driver's photo, name, and vehicle plate number 2 hours before pickup.

Payments & Billing

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, UnionPay (中国银联), and PayPal. Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We never store your full card number or CVV.
If your trip runs over the booked time or waiting time exceeds the free allowance, overtime is charged at the vehicle's hourly rate, billed per minute. Overtime fees are automatically calculated and charged to your payment method after the trip, and you'll receive an email with a detailed breakdown.
